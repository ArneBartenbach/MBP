Welcome!

The MPB is here to help you manage your IoT environments!

You can set up your IoT environment in four steps:

 Step 1 - Create extraction/control operators for your sensors and actuators.

 Step 2 - Start modeling your IoT environment with the MBP modeling tool.

 Step 3 - Register backup your modeled IoT environment.

 Step 4 - Deploy sync your modeled IoT environment.

Now you can access your devices, sensors and actuators!
